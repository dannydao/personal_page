From South Korea, Han Sunwoo aka "Jett" is a Radiant with powers to control wind. The 10th and youngest member to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL, Jett is an independent, confident individual that prefers to do things her own way. Though viewed as having a "toxic" attitude to other people, Jett's in game voicelines indicate that she does care about those close to her and may just not know how to communicate her feelings properly.
